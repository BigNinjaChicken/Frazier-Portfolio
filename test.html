My Fifth Bulldog

Meet my fifth bulldog!

 This is a very long description, which describes the origins of my fifth bulldog. It was a dark, stormy night when my fifth bulldog came to me. I sat inside in my rocking chair, listening to the wind howl, when I heard a different, faint howl outside my door. Unbeknownst to me, my fifth bulldog was waiting for me on my front porch. I let him inside, and we have been best friends since.
